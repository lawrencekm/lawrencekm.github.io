About Retriever


Having established itself in no less than eleven Sub-Saharan countries and with more than two hundred and thirty thousand vehicles already on its system both locally and internationally, Retriever is proud to boast one of the highest recovery rates in the vehicle tracking industry. Offering a variety of stolen vehicle recovery and fleet management packages, Retriever not only employs state-of-the-art GSM and satellite tracking technology, but also strives to provide its customers with superior service and total peace of mind


Developed by some of the most experienced players in the tracking and fleet management industry, the Retriever vehicle recovery system has superior functionality, is extremely reliable and very affordable. Retriever also offers the corporate client a choice of either basic or comprehensive fleet monitoring services. These are designed to provide effective solutions for the operation, control and management of fleets. The Retriever system was originally developed and implemented for the Sub-Saharan market where vehicle theft was, and still is, amongst the highest and most sophisticated in the world.
